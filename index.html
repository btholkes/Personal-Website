Who am I?

If you couldn't guess this already, my name is Braden Tholkes. I am a Parks and Recreation Management major at Western Carolina University. I am focusing my studies around Outdoor Leadership and Instruction, because I hope to be a climbing guide or NOLS/Outward Bound instructor. My life goal is to get as many people reconnected with nature as I can! My biggest passion is climbing, but I adore any activity that gets me outside, and welcome any chance to have new experiences in new areas. I started my college career as a Computer Science major, and studied the art of wasting away infront of a screen for two years before rediscovering my childhood passion for the outdoors. I firmly believe the outdoors is where I belong, and I am happier than I have ever been climbing every weekend, and studying classes that prepare me for stoking the passions of others for the outdoors.
